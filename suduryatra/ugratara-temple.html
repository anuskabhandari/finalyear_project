Ugratara Temple - Details

Overview

Ugratara Temple is one of the most sacred Hindu temples located in Dadeldhura, Nepal. It is dedicated to Goddess Tara and draws thousands of pilgrims during major festivals. Surrounded by hills and peaceful atmosphere, it is both a religious and scenic destination.

Details

Location:Dadeldhura District, Sudurpashchim Nepal
Entry Fee:Free
Best Time to Visit:Dashain and other major Hindu festivals
Main Attractions:Goddess Tara shrine, cultural rituals, scenic views
Recommended Duration:1 day
