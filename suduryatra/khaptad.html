Khaptad National Park - Details

Overview

Khaptad National Park is a peaceful, spiritual and naturally rich destination in the far western hills of Nepal. It offers scenic landscapes, a variety of flora and fauna, and religious significance due to Khaptad Baba. Perfect for trekking, meditation, and nature walks.

Details

Location:Far-Western Nepal (Doti, Bajura, Bajhang & Achham)
Entry Fee:Rs. 100 per person
Best Time to Visit:March to May, September to November
Main Attractions:Khaptad Baba Ashram, grasslands, medicinal herbs
Recommended Duration:3-4 days
